What is our R&D case study service?

This service is designed for companies that either know or are unsure if they have conducted R&D and have not claimed the tax benefit for the investment of associated time and resources (claimable for up to 24 months post activities). Some companies do not have the specialist resource available in-house to draft a strong supporting report for their tax claim and require our support to pull this together.

Should you wish to speak to any of our clients, we can provide references.

The full service covers...

1.

Undertaking primary research within the organisation to determine the qualifying R&D activities.

2.

Gathering supporting evidence and ensuring qualification compliance with tax rules against the definition of R&D.

3.

Writing your supporting business case and submitting this to your end of year accountant.

4.

Helping you collate financial qualifying expenditure in monetary value and submission to your end of year accountant.

Competitive fixed sum pricing can be provided on application.
