Award Submissions

Helping you write the best possible award entry.

Omnia Freelance write a number of award entries for a wide range of businesses every year. Rachel Johnson, who leads our freelance service, has penned multiple market leading entries for a number of shortlisted and winning companies.

What is our awards writing service?

Omnia freelance offer an end to end writing service for any companies interested in submitting an entry for an industry award. Over 8 out of every 10 award submissions we have written over the last 3 years have been shortlisted and many have gone on to win in their chosen categories.

If you want to speak to any of our award winning clients, we can provide references.

This service is designed for those that have an achievement to celebrate but feel that they do not have the in-house or agency resources to handle the tight copy deadlines and the demands of putting together a strong award entry.

The full service covers the following activity:

  1. Taking a verbal brief on the project/person/team/initiative that you would like to submit for an award
  2. Consulting as to which category or categories to submit entries for and ensuring compliance with the competition rules
  3. Writing and gaining your approval on award entries
  4. Gathering and submitting supporting evidence
  5. Chasing in customer advocacy quotes
  6. Submitting the entry/entries on your behalf

Pricing on application.

Those submitting entries for more than one category using this service will be entitled to a discount on the total cost.
